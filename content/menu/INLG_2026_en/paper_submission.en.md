## Call for Papers – Peer-Reviewed International Workshop

The AIWolfDial2026 workshop invites submissions of peer-reviewed papers.
Topics are not limited to AIWolf intelligence; submissions on LLM-based dialogue generation and automated game playing in general are also welcome.
We plan to notify authors of the AIWolf International Contest evaluation results before the paper submission deadline, so you will be able to write your paper based on those results.
Note that paper submission is not required to participate in the contest, but it is strongly encouraged.
Presentations will be held in a hybrid format combining in-person and online participation.

## Page Limits and Format

Following INLG guidelines, we accept both long papers (8 pages, excluding appendices and references) and short papers (4 pages, same exclusion).
Manuscripts should follow the ACL format. Please refer to the official INLG website for details:
https://2026.inlgmeeting.org/calls.html
